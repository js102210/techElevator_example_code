One of the things that makes the Avatar universe so compelling is that the various ethnic groups and events have real-world allegories that are expressed through aesthetic and thematic choices both overt and subtle. For example, consider the following table containing the four fictional world powers, the actual cultures from our world they likely represent, and supporting evidence for the connections.

Elemental Nation Allegories

Tend to live in structures built atop or into the side of mountains, similar to actual Tibetan monks Katara's hairstyle is based on an Inuit hairstyle Royal Hairstyles resembles those of East Asian nobility Goverment of the interior cities of the earth kingdom is similar to stereotyping of Chinese government-- fiercely communist, populace controlled through secret police and propoganda
